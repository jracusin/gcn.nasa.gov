The GCN Team regularly presents updates at topical scientific conferences and meetings, and interacts with the broader astronomy community via a table at the NASA booth at the American Astronomical Society winter meetings. Our booth includes an [educational, interactive demonstration](https://github.com/nasa-gcn/gcn-raspberry-pi-demo) of the fault-tolerant Apache Kafka data streaming technology behind GCN.
